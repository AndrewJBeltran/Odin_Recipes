The Best Lasagna You'll Ever Have

Look at that beautiful piece of lasagna, it's also very delicious.

    Ingredients
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • One Can of Crushed tomatoes.
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • White Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Parsley, Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • A single egg

    Step by Step:
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order: Meat Sauce - Noodles - Ricotta mixture - Mozzarella Slices - Meat Sauce - Parmesan - Repeat
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees F for 25 minutes. Remove foil after 25 minutes and bake again for another 25 minutes.
  6. Let Lasagna rest for 15 minutes at room temperature.
