The Freshest and Tastiest Lemonade!

Looks tasty doesn't it? Now lets try to make some!

    Ingredients:
  • Sugar and Water (simple sugar mix)
  • Lemons
  • Ice

    Step-by-Step:
  1. Juice the Lemons
  2. Make Simple Syrup: Mix water and sugar in sauce pan and bring to boil. Stir the mixture until the sugar dissolves
  3. Pour cold water into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp. Then add the simple syrup and continue stirring
  4. Serve in cups with ice!
